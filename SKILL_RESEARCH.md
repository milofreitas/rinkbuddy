## SECTION 1: FOUNDATIONS (Learn to Skate USA Basic Skills 1-6)

### Basic 1 — First Steps
- Sit on ice and stand up
- March forward across the ice
- Forward two-foot glide
- Dip (deep knee bend while gliding)
- Forward swizzles (6-8 in a row)
- Backward wiggles (6-8 in a row)
- Beginning snowplow stop (one or both feet)
- Two-foot hop in place (bonus)

### Basic 2 — Building Confidence
- Scooter pushes (R and L)
- Forward one-foot glides (R and L)
- Backward two-foot glide
- Rocking horse (one forward swizzle + one backward swizzle, repeat)
- Backward swizzles (6-8 in a row)
- Two-foot turns forward to backward in place (CW and CCW)
- Moving snowplow stop
- Curves (bonus)

### Basic 3 — FUNdamentals Start
- Beginning forward stroking (correct blade use)
- Forward half swizzle pumps on circle (6-8, both directions)
- Moving forward-to-backward two-foot turns on circle (both directions)
- Beginning backward one-foot glides
- Backward snowplow stop (R and L)
- Forward slalom
- Forward pivots CW and CCW (bonus)

### Basic 4 — Edges & Crossovers
- Forward outside edge on circle (R and L)
- Forward inside edge on circle (R and L)
- Forward crossovers (CW and CCW)
- Backward half swizzle pumps on circle (both directions)
- Backward one-foot glides (R and L)
- Beginning two-foot spin (2-4 revolutions)
- Forward lunges both legs (bonus)

### Basic 5 — Intermediate Foundations
- Backward outside edge on circle (R and L)
- Backward inside edge on circle (R and L)
- Backward crossovers (CW and CCW)
- Forward outside three-turn (R and L)
- Advanced two-foot spin (4-6 revolutions)
- Hockey stop (both directions)
- Side toe hop R and L (bonus)

### Basic 6 — Advanced Foundations
- Forward inside three-turn (R and L)
- Moving backward-to-forward two-foot turn on circle (both directions)
- Backward stroking
- Beginning one-foot spin (2-4 revolutions)
- T-stops (R and L)
- Bunny hop
- Forward spiral on straight line (R or L)
- Shoot the duck R or L (bonus)

## SECTION 2: FIGURE SKATING ELEMENTS

### Jumps (ISU recognized, ordered by difficulty)

#### Toe Jumps (use toe pick for takeoff):
| Jump | Takeoff | Landing | Single BV | Double BV | Triple BV | Quad BV |
|------|---------|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Toe Loop (T) | Back outside edge + toe | Back outside edge | 0.4 | 1.3 | 4.2 | 9.5 |
| Flip (F) | Back inside edge + toe | Back outside edge | 0.5 | 1.8 | 5.3 | 11.0 |
| Lutz (Lz) | Back outside edge + toe | Back outside edge | 0.6 | 2.1 | 5.9 | 11.5 |

#### Edge Jumps (no toe pick):
| Jump | Takeoff | Landing | Single BV | Double BV | Triple BV | Quad BV |
|------|---------|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Salchow (S) | Back inside edge | Back outside edge | 0.4 | 1.3 | 4.3 | 9.7 |
| Loop (Lo) | Back outside edge | Back outside edge | 0.5 | 1.7 | 4.9 | 10.5 |
| Axel (A) | Forward outside edge | Back outside edge | 1.1 | 3.3 | 8.0 | 12.5 |
| Euler (Eu) | Back outside edge | Back inside edge | 0.5 | — | — | — |

#### Other Jumps:
- Waltz Jump — half-rotation from forward outside edge (0.3 BV)
- Bunny Hop — simple hop, no rotation
- Half Loop / Euler — connects jumps in combinations
- Quintuple jumps — 14.0 BV (all types, added 2024-25)

### Spins

#### Three Basic Positions:
1. **Upright Spin (USp)** — Extended skating leg, not camel
2. **Sit Spin (SSp)** — Squatting, knee bend < 90 degrees
3. **Camel Spin (CSp)** — Free leg extended back, knee above hip

#### Upright Variations:
- Scratch spin — free leg crossed at ankle
- Back scratch spin — on opposite foot
- Layback spin (LSp) — head/shoulders dropped back, back arched
- Biellmann spin — free foot held over head
- "I" spin — free leg held vertical in front (splits)
- "Y" spin — free leg held vertical to side
- Pearl spin — catchfoot layback to Biellmann
- Haircutter — free foot brought toward head

#### Sit Spin Variations:
- Basic sit spin
- Back sit spin
- Broken leg — free leg turned inward at knee
- Pancake — free leg crossed horizontal over skating leg
- Cannonball — arms down touching skating leg

#### Camel Spin Variations:
- Basic camel
- Layover camel — torso and leg point to ceiling
- Catch-foot camel — holding free foot blade
- Donut spin — catch-foot with circular shape
- Illusion spin — body shifts up and down rhythmically

#### Spin Modifiers:
- Flying entry (F prefix) — entered through a jump
- Change foot (C prefix)
- Combination spin (CoSp) — multiple positions
- Death drop — flying entry into back sit spin
- Butterfly — flying entry into camel

#### ISU Spin Abbreviations:
- USp, FUSp, CUSp = Upright
- SSp, FSSp, CSSp = Sit
- CSp, FCSp, CCSp = Camel
- LSp, FLSp, CLSp = Layback
- CoSp, FCoSp, CCoSp = Combination
- Levels: B, 1, 2, 3, 4

### Turns (One-Foot)

| Turn | Edge Change | Curve Change | Direction |
|------|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Three Turn | Yes (in→out or out→in) | No (same lobe) | With the curve |
| Bracket | Yes (in→out or out→in) | No (same lobe) | Against the curve |
| Rocker | No (maintains edge) | Yes (opposite curve) | Into the entry curve |
| Counter | No (maintains edge) | Yes (opposite curve) | Against the entry curve |
| Twizzle | Traveling, one+ rotation | N/A | Either direction |

### Turns (Two-Foot)

| Turn | Edge Change | Description |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Mohawk | No (same edge maintained) | Change of foot, forward↔backward |
| Choctaw | Yes (edge changes) | Change of foot AND edge, forward↔backward |

Mohawk types: Open, Closed, Swing
Choctaw types: Open, Closed, Cross-open

### Step Sequences (StSq)
- Levels B through 4
- Must utilize full ice surface
- Requires variety of difficult turns: brackets, rockers, counters, choctaws, twizzles
- Level 4 requires: double twizzle, bracket, rocker, counter, choctaw, outside mohawk

### Choreographic Sequences (ChSq1)
- Fixed at Level 1
- Can include: steps, turns, spirals, arabesques, spread eagles, Ina Bauers, hydroblading, transitional jumps, spinning movements

### Field Moves / Spirals
- Forward spiral (arabesque position on one foot)
- Back spiral
- Inside spiral / Outside spiral
- Charlotte spiral (body fully inverted)
- Fan spiral
- Catch-foot spiral
- Spread eagle (both feet on ice, turned out)
- Ina Bauer (one foot forward, one back, knees bent)
- Hydroblading (deep edge, body nearly parallel to ice)

### Pair Elements
- Side-by-side jumps
- Throw jumps (TTh, STh, LoTh, FTh, LzTh, ATh)
- Pair spins (PSp, PCoSp)
- Death spirals (BiDs, BoDs, FiDs, FoDs)
- Lifts (Groups 1-5: 1Li-5ALi)
- Twist lifts (Tw)

### Ice Dance Elements
- Pattern dances (PSt)
- Step sequences (DiSt, MiSt, CiSt, SeSt)
- One-foot turns (OFTW/OFTM)
- Dance spins (DSp)
- Dance lifts (StaLi, SlLi, CuLi, RoLi)
- Twizzles (SqTw, SyTw)
- Choreographic elements (ChLi1, ChSp1, ChTw1, ChSl1, ChSt1, ChAJ1, ChHy1, ChRS1)
