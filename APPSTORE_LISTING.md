### Description (4000 chars max)
RinkBuddy is the ultimate companion for ice skaters who want to track, analyze, and improve their performance on the ice. Whether you're a competitive figure skater training for your next competition, a hockey player working on skating drills, or a recreational skater looking to level up, RinkBuddy gives you the tools to measure your progress and reach your goals.

RECORD AND REVIEW YOUR SESSIONS
Use your phone's camera to record skating sessions directly in the app. Capture jumps, spins, footwork sequences, and skating drills from any angle. Review your footage frame by frame to identify areas for improvement and celebrate your wins.

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS OVER TIME
Log every practice session and competition. RinkBuddy keeps a detailed history of your skating journey so you can see how far you've come. Track the skills you're working on, set personal goals, and monitor your improvement week over week.

ORGANIZE YOUR SKATING LIFE
Keep all your skating data in one place. Track your rink visits, practice hours, lesson notes, and competition results. RinkBuddy helps you stay organized so you can focus on what matters most: your skating.

BUILT FOR SKATERS, BY SKATERS
RinkBuddy was designed with input from competitive figure skaters, coaches, and skating enthusiasts. Every feature is built to solve real problems that skaters face in their training.

KEY FEATURES:
- Video recording and playback for session review
- Session logging and practice tracking
- Progress tracking with detailed history
- Goal setting and milestone tracking
- Clean, intuitive interface designed for use at the rink
- Works offline so you can use it anywhere, even in cold rinks with spotty WiFi

Whether you skate once a week or train every day, RinkBuddy helps you make the most of your time on the ice. Download now and start tracking your skating journey today.
